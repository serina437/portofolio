Zoo Camp Activities

At Zoo Camp, you will not only enjoy entertainment but also gain valuable experiences with your family! Each activity is designed to provide excitement while deepening your knowledge of the animal world.
Starting with the amazing Animal Show, where you can see animals in action, feeding your favorite animals in Feeding Animal, and participating in Tour Guide Education to learn more about the lives of animals and their habitats.

Zoo Camp Animals

Meerkat

small, swift, and ever-alert, this desert scout stands tall on sandy lookouts, always protecting its clan by taking turns as ‘guards’ to spot predators from afar

Lemur

With my big eyes and playful spirit, I leap through the trees, thriving in my social groups while communicating through a variety of calls

Zebra

Striking black-and-white stripes define me as I graze in herds across the savanna, blending beauty with social harmony.

Tapir

Gentle and unique, I navigate the rainforest with my long snout, playing a key role in maintaining my habitat's balance.

Peacock

With my dazzling fan of iridescent feathers, I strut proudly to showcase my beauty, captivating all who behold me.

Flamingo

Balancing elegantly on one leg, I’m a vibrant pink beauty, wading through shallow waters as I filter-feed on my favorite crustaceans.

Owl

the wise night hunter with captivating eyes! Known for its silent flight and exceptional hearing, this enigmatic bird prowls the darkness, ready to catch its prey under the moonlight.

Parrot

a colorful chatterbox of the rainforest! With its vibrant feathers and ability to mimic sounds, this intelligent bird brightens up any jungle with playful antics and lively conversations.

Iguana

Iguana: a cool reptilian sunbather with a spiky crest! This herbivorous lizard thrives in tropical habitats, often lounging on branches and blending seamlessly into its leafy surroundings.

Crocodile

A fierce predator lurking in the water! With powerful jaws and stealth, this reptile blends into its surroundings, waiting for the perfect moment to strike.

Chameleon

The color-changing master of disguise! This unique lizard adapts its hues to blend in, showcasing its incredible camouflage skills.

Red Panda

Small and playful, I swing through trees, munching on bamboo while showcasing my adorable rust-colored fur.

Tiger

As the jungle's majestic ruler, my striking stripes and stealthy grace make me a powerful hunter and a symbol of beauty.

Tiger

I’m a clever and social explorer, using my intelligence and playful spirit to thrive in my tight-knit family.

Giraffe

With my long neck and gentle nature, I gracefully reach for leaves high in the trees, embodying the beauty of the savanna.

Panda

As a cuddly ambassador for conservation, I spend my days munching on bamboo, promoting peace and harmony in my forest home

Rhino

Strong yet gentle, I roam the grasslands, playing a vital role in maintaining my habitat and protecting the wild.

Orangutan

Swinging through the rainforest, my intelligence and dexterity shine as I use tools and navigate my arboreal world.
